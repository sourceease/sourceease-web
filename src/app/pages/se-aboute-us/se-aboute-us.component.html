On-line Global Talent Hiring Market Place.

UrJobMart is a marketplace, where we intend to bring buyers and suppliers to the same platform. This platform also allows organizations to completely outsource their Talent Acquisition process, including Interview and Assessment process to the platform. With certified Interviewers and very detailed and relevant assessment tools – our clients can be assured of a very thorough fitment assessment. With a combination of in-house (UrJobMart) and 3rd party recruiters, our clients can be assured of scaling their Talent Acquisition needs without having to worry about spike in the budget or finding new consultants to meet their demand – as the portal allows to scale up or down based on the need without any commitment.

It is a complete & comprehensive alternative to the way the industry is functioning today to onboard talent that can be customized to suit your style of functioning.

Goal to make the human capital acquisition a simple, predictable & a repeatable process.

The product was conceptualized by industry veterans with over 80+ years of combined industry experience. The roadmap over a period of next year or so is very ambitious. Not only do we intend to provide simplification & transparency in the talent acquisition process but access to AI based tools for prescriptive and predictive analytics and real-time dashboards.
